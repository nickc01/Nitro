    CombinablePowerup

    A powerup that can have its effects combined with other powerups. When several powerups are collected via a MultiplePowerupCollector, they will form a chain that is sorted based on the Priority of the powerups.

    The powerups with the highest priority will be executed first, while the ones with the lowest priority will be executed last. When a powerup is executed, it will have it's Execute(CombinablePowerup, Vector3, Quaternion, Action<Vector3, Quaternion>) function called.

    With the execute function, you will be able to know the previous powerup in the chain, the position and rotation of where the powerup is affecting, and a delegate called runNextPowerup, which when called, will execute the next powerup in the chain. You can use this delegate to control where and when the next powerups in the chain are executed"/>
